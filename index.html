For the Trickster, it is more than just music.

Being independent, and inspired by both personal hardship and his role as a global business leader, Trickster sees the world in a unique and compelling way. This view is born not out of learned ideology but of true, real-world experience. Having witnessed suffering in all four corners of the world, Trickster knows that now is the time to make change real.

For Trickster Real Change is no Joke.

To make change happen, we need to address not only the need, but also how we live and continue to thrive on this earth. The earth of which we are the custodians, not owners of. The sooner we all understand that the better.
